Instructions for Aquachloro calculator

Input instructions

You have to consider the nature of your raw chlorine before using this app. Is it a solid compound, liquid or gaseous? What is the percentage purity of the chlorine compound you are using for your water treatment? What is the volume of the raw water which you are trying to chlorinate? These information will enable you to provide proper input. Where your water has low contaminant, your chlorine demand might be very low, hence, there is no much challenge in the dozing process. However, where your water source is highly loaded, consider chlorine demand before you can know the required dose.
