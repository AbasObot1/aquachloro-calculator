Aquachloro calculator disclaimer

The Aquachloro calculator is intended as a general estimation tool to assist with pool and spa chlorine treatment calculations. It should not be considered a substitute for the professional advice of a qualified pool service technician or chemist.

Here are some important limitations to consider:

Accuracy: The accuracy of the calculations provided by the Aquachloro calculator depends on the accuracy of the information you input.

Local Regulations: Chlorine application rates may be subject to local regulations. Always consult your local authorities for any specific requirements.

Environmental Factors: Environmental factors such as sunlight, temperature, and bather load can significantly impact chlorine demand. The Aquachloro calculator may not account for all these variables.

Professional Expertise: For complex pool or spa problems, or for ensuring ongoing water quality and safety, consulting with a qualified pool service technician or chemist is strongly recommended.
